Welcome to AlumniMatch!
Thank you for visiting! AlumniMatch instantly matches and connects you with other college alumni from your past college life in a free and easy to use app! The app is invite only and creates private networks for each alumni community.

Members are matched based by shared:

  • Past College Degrees
  • Past Student Orgs
  • Hobbies & Interests
  • Favorite Games to Play
  • Industries & Work Needs
  • Spirituality & Religion
  • Relationship Desires
  • and so much more!
Imagine who you will meet on AlumniMatch!

Your Privacy: AlumniMatch never shares your information outside of the platform, and members can easily remove their account anytime. Learn more at www.alumnimatch.co.
